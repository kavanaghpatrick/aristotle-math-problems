# Erdős Problem #23 - Case n=2

## THE PROBLEM

Every triangle-free graph on 5n vertices can be made bipartite by deleting at most n² edges.

For n=2: Every triangle-free graph on 10 vertices can be made bipartite by deleting at most 4 edges.

## PRIOR SUCCESS

The n=1 case has been PROVEN (verified, compiles):
```lean
theorem erdos_problem_23_case_n_1
  {V : Type} [Fintype V] [DecidableEq V] (G : SimpleGraph V) [DecidableRel G.Adj]
  (h_card : Fintype.card V = 5) (h_tf : G.CliqueFree 3) :
  ∃ (s : Finset (Sym2 V)), s ⊆ G.edgeFinset ∧ s.card ≤ 1 ∧ (G.deleteEdges s).Colorable 2
```
