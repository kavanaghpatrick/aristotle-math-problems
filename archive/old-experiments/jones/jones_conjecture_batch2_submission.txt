## PD Code Format

PD codes represent knot diagrams as crossing lists: `[[a,b,c,d], ...]`
- Each crossing: `[a,b,c,d]` where `a,b,c,d` are strand labels in counter-clockwise order
- Strand `b` crosses OVER strand `d` at a positive crossing
- Standard format used by KnotInfo database

**Validation**: Can verify against KnotInfo database values for known Jones polynomials.
