# Fable + Aristotle Attack Plan — 2026-06-09

Synthesized from the evidence brief at `analysis/fable_aristotle_evidence_brief_20260609.md` (4 DB miners + 3 web researchers), 5 confirmed attack vectors with adversarial verifier refinements, and 6 rejected vectors. Governing directive from the project owner: **Fable thinks hard, debates, and researches to brute-force candidate SOLUTIONS to the problems themselves; Aristotle then closes the remaining gap or formally adjudicates whether the proposed solution is correct — verification OR refutation, and a disproof counts.**

## 1. Executive Summary

**The baseline is zero.** 1253 submissions across 570 problems have produced 0 `compiled_advance` rows: 805 `compiled_no_advance`, 262 `compile_failed`, 133 `compiled_partial`, 17 `disproven`, 36 nominally in flight. 93.2% of that volume (1168/1253) went down the bare lane (bare conjecture → MCGS formalizer), a mode with 0/1168 advances here and zero documented genuine open-problem solves anywhere in the public record. The instrument was wrong, not just the aim: Aristotle's formalizer never discovers a witness for a bare existential (confirmed at slot724), its verified-technique corpus is entirely finite computation (`decide` 208 uses, `native_decide` 123), and its ceiling is "a sharp Mathlib contributor's day" — it substitutes elementary folklore for deep machinery 5/6 times and executes deep machinery 0/6.

**What changes with Fable.** Every externally credible AI resolution of an Erdős problem follows one pattern — a strong reasoner AUTHORS the mathematics, a prover ADJUDICATES it: E728 (GPT-5.2 Pro authored, Aristotle formalized, arXiv:2601.07421), E650 (arXiv:2603.28636), E848 (arXiv:2511.16072), E1196 (one autonomous GPT-5.4 Pro query with a novel technique, arXiv:2605.00301). This project has never once run that pattern with a frontier author: 0/107 cross-domain LLM consultations invented anything, 87% were meta-process, and the informal lane (0/67 but 30% partial — 3x the bare scaffold rate) was always one-shot and abandoned. Fable 5 is the first author in this pipeline whose model lineage has independently reproduced a genuine open-problem disproof (E90 unit-distance, arXiv:2605.20695, replicated by Claude Mythos). The campaign therefore centers ONE loop: **Fable authors candidate solutions — complete proof arguments, explicit witnesses, counterexample constructions, found by hard thinking, multi-agent debate, and exhaustive search — and Aristotle (or the Lean kernel directly) adjudicates each candidate: verify or refute.** Aristotle is never again asked to discover; refutations are logged to `false_lemmas` as loop progress, not failure.

**Calibrated expectations.** Tao's framing holds: easy-tail Erdős problems are the AI sweet spot; deep open problems remain at ~0%. DeepMind's best agent ran ~2.5% (9/353 open Erdős at ~$300/problem, arXiv:2605.22763); our realistic ceiling with every fix applied is 3-5% diluted, perhaps 5-10% on the authoring-amenable subset. Residual problems are adversely selected (Bloom: "surprisingly few have fallen"). **"Groundbreaking" for this campaign means exactly ONE genuine resolution or publishable disproof of an open Erdős problem** — statement-faithful, Lean-verified, literature-diffed, honestly scored. Five deep-focus targets, eight weeks (2026-06-09 → 2026-08-04; week 1 closes 2026-06-15), pre-registered kill criteria on every branch. The modal outcome of any single branch is an honest negative result; the portfolio exists so that one jackpot pays for all tickets.

## 4. The Fable–Aristotle Interaction Protocol (the Author–Adjudicate Loop)

This is the owner-directed core. One loop, eight stages, audit gates between every pair. Aristotle's role throughout is ADJUDICATOR: it verifies or refutes Fable-authored mathematics. A refutation is a successful loop iteration — the refuted claim enters `false_lemmas`, the search space shrinks, Fable re-authors.

**Stage A — Target lock + interpretation audit.** Fable diffs the formal Lean statement verbatim against the source (erdosproblems.com prose via grok-search, formal-conjectures upstream). The audit predicate (what theorem shape counts as `target_resolved=1`) is pre-registered in the DB BEFORE any work — this is what killed slot724-style restated-target mirages and the E124 mis-statement trap. Em-tautology gate (`_looks_like_em_tautology()`) and the `(P)∨¬P` ban apply.

**Stage B — Research + debate.** Fable checks `mk failed`, `false_lemmas`, `failed_approaches`, prior result files (READ the Lean — never trust `sorry_count`; 5 of 8 recent 1-sorry rows were restated targets). Cross-domain literature ≤2 years attached per the F2 rule. For nontrivial strategy forks, `/debate` (Grok + Gemini + Codex) and spawned Fable subagents argue the branch; the Bloom rule applies — if Fable can author the full proof in one pass, treat the target as retrieval-suspect and literature-gate FIRST.

**Stage C — Solution brute-force (Fable authors).** Three artifact shapes, all "candidate solutions to the problem itself": (i) a complete informal proof argument (obstruction memo → repaired argument, V1); (ii) an explicit witness object found by search Fable designs and runs (SAT model, sieve hit — V2/V3/V4); (iii) a counterexample construction. Search harnesses count as authoring: Fable writes the encoder/sieve AND an independent re-verifier against the original predicate, never trusting the harness's own bookkeeping.

**Stage D — Adversarial self-review.** Two rounds by a spawned Fable grader subagent with the explicit brief "refute this argument." Kill criteria pre-registered in the memo fire here, not after spend. Only artifacts surviving review proceed.

**Stage E — Adjudication submit.** Routing by artifact shape: decide-shaped witnesses → LOCAL lake build first (codex-prove loop); Aristotle optional notary/simplifier. Authored informal arguments → the Method-1 pipeline: `method1_loop.py enqueue --problem-id <id>` then `run --live` (no `--informal-mode` flag exists on method1_loop), with the `.fusion.json` companion carrying the memo + literature dossier as `informal_proof_outline`; safe_submit's I9 routing turns that outline into the full-argument informal prompt (E728 protocol, subsystem #2), and the row lands `lane='informal'` per the V1 pre-registration. method1_loop's admission gate enforces the artifact requirement mechanically: no outline → REFUSED. The ask to Aristotle is always adjudicative: prove this argument's gap, or exhibit where it fails. Disproof counts — an Aristotle counterexample to a Fable lemma is a deliverable.

**Stage F — Fetch + read, immediately.** Every job fetched within 48h of completion (`scripts/aristotle_fetch.py`). Timestamp convention for the SLA: the 48h clock starts at the job's Harmonic-reported completion timestamp in UTC, as surfaced by `aristotle_fetch.py status` polling — not at submission and not at local notice. V0's lesson is that the loop died from unread outputs. Fable reads the returned Lean line-by-line: classify every sorry as mirage (restated target) / narrow-genuine / hard-core; check for smuggled axioms, `exact em _`, bound-injection, type-escape.

**Stage G — Repair loop.** Discrepancies route to the REASONER, never the prover (E728 protocol; F3 lesson — 87% of past LLM use was meta-process because strategy never reached the author). Fable re-authors the failed step; resubmission goes through `Project.ask()` on the SAME persistent project, keeping proven lemmas (arXiv:2510.01346 §2.2.2), resuming on `OUT_OF_BUDGET`. Note: the two prior `ask()` ITER2 follow-ups (ids 1254/1255) produced no advance — the loop is only credited when paired with a re-authored argument, not a bare retry. Finish every success with a simplify pass.

**Stage H — Audit gauntlet (anti-fake-advance).** Before any status upgrade: (1) statement diff vs Stage-A pre-registration; (2) axiom audit (`audit_proven.py`; `axiomatizes_prior_work=0`); (3) `mathematical_content_score` honestly populated (the F1 pathology: 57% of historical "advances" were bounded extensions — bounded verification is `compiled_no_advance` by default); (4) post-hit literature re-diff before the words "resolution" or "disproof" are used anywhere; (5) `compiled_advance` stays opt-in: `contribution_statement` + `target_resolved=1` + mcs, all three. Rivin's number (97.6% proved, 67% semantically correct) is the standing reason this stage exists.
