OPEN GAP: Erdos 274 — Herzog-Schonheim Conjecture
Source: formal-conjectures/FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/274.lean
Domain: algebra

If G is exactly covered by cosets of subgroups G_1,...,G_k with k > 1, must two indices [G:G_i] coincide?

theorem herzog_schonheim {G : Type*} [Group G] (hG : 1 < ENat.card G)
    {ι : Type*} [Fintype ι] (hι : 1 < Fintype.card ι)
    (P : Group.ExactCovering G ι) :
    ∃ i j, i ≠ j ∧ (P.parts i).index = (P.parts j).index := by sorry

Status: OPEN. Proved for finite abelian groups. The general group case remains open.
