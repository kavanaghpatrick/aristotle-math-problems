OPEN GAP: Kurepa's Conjecture
Source: Kurepa (1971)
Domain: nt

For every odd prime p, the left factorial !p = 0! + 1! + ... + (p-1)!
is not divisible by p.

Equivalently: !p ≢ 0 (mod p) for all odd primes p.

By Wilson's theorem, (p-1)! ≡ -1 (mod p). Partial sums of factorials
modulo p do not exhibit periodicity that would force divisibility.
Verified computationally for all primes up to 10^6 (Barsky-Benzaghou).

Barsky-Benzaghou (2004) proved: !p ≡ 1 + (p-1)/2 · p (mod p²)
for p ≥ 3, which gives !p ≢ 0 (mod p) iff (p+1)/2 ≢ 0 (mod p),
true for p ≥ 3. WAIT — this would prove it. Need to verify.
