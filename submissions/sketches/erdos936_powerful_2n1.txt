OPEN GAP: Erdős Problem 936 — Finitely Many Powerful 2^n + 1
Source: Erdős Problem 936
Domain: nt

There are only finitely many n such that 2^n + 1 is powerful
(every prime factor appears with exponent ≥ 2).

Only known powerful value: 2^3 + 1 = 9 = 3².
Conditionally follows from ABC conjecture.
No unconditional proof known.

theorem erdos936 : Set.Finite
    {n : ℕ | n ≥ 1 ∧
      ∀ p : ℕ, Nat.Prime p → p ∣ (2 ^ n + 1) → p ^ 2 ∣ (2 ^ n + 1)}
    := by sorry

Status: OPEN. Only 2³+1=9 is powerful. ABC implies finiteness.
