OPEN GAP: Erdos 672 — AP products not perfect cubes
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/672.lean
Domain: nt

The product of 4 or more terms in arithmetic progression with
first term n, common difference d, gcd(n,d)=1, is never a
perfect cube (or higher perfect power).

The l=2 case is known (Euler/Oblath). The l >= 3 case is open.

theorem erdos_672_l3 :
    ∀ n d : ℕ, n > 0 → d > 0 → n.gcd d = 1 →
    ¬ ∃ y : ℕ, (∏ i in Finset.range 4, (n + i * d)) = y ^ 3 := by
  sorry

Status: OPEN. Product of 4 AP terms (coprime) is never a cube.
