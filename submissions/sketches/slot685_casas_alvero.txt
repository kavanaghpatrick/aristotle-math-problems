OPEN GAP: Casas-Alvero conjecture
Source: formal-conjectures/FormalConjectures/Paper/CasasAlvero.lean
Domain: algebra

In characteristic 0, if monic polynomial P shares a common factor with each of its
Hasse derivatives up to order deg(P)-1, then P = (X - α)^d for some α.

theorem casas_alvero_conjecture (P : K[X]) (hP : Monic P) (hP' : HasCasasAlveroProp P) :
    ∃ α : K, P = (X - C α) ^ P.natDegree := by sorry

Status: OPEN. Known for degrees p^k and 2p^k (p prime). False in positive characteristic.
