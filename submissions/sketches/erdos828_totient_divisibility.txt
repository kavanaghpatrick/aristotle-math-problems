OPEN GAP: Erdős Problem 828 — Infinitely Many φ(n) | (n+1)
Source: Erdős Problem 828
Domain: nt

Are there infinitely many n with φ(n) | (n + 1)?

Known solutions: n = 1, 2, 4, 8, 12. The full characterization of
which integers a give infinitely many solutions to φ(n) | (n + a)
is open. Related to Lehmer's conjecture (a = 0 case).
