OPEN GAP: Erdos 850 — Existence of same-radical triple-shift pair
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/850.lean
Domain: nt

Do there exist distinct positive x, y such that rad(x)=rad(y),
rad(x+1)=rad(y+1), and rad(x+2)=rad(y+2)?

theorem erdos_850_existence :
    ∃ x y : ℕ, 0 < x ∧ 0 < y ∧ x ≠ y
    ∧ x.primeFactors = y.primeFactors
    ∧ (x+1).primeFactors = (y+1).primeFactors
    ∧ (x+2).primeFactors = (y+2).primeFactors := by sorry

Status: OPEN. No witness is known. Aristotle must either exhibit a pair
or refute by proving the negation (use erdos850_impossibility.txt for that side).
