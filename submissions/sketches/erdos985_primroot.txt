OPEN GAP: Erdos 985 — Primitive root smaller than p
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/985.lean
Domain: nt

For every odd prime p, there exists a prime q < p
that is a primitive root modulo p.

theorem erdos_985 :
    ∀ p : ℕ, p.Prime → p ≠ 2 →
    ∃ q : ℕ, q.Prime ∧ q < p ∧
    orderOf (q : ZMod p) = p - 1 := by sorry

Status: OPEN. Related to Artin's conjecture. Heath-Brown
proved at least one of {2,3,5} is primitive root for inf primes.
