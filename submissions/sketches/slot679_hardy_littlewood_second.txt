OPEN GAP: Second Hardy-Littlewood conjecture
Source: formal-conjectures/FormalConjectures/Wikipedia/HardyLittlewood.lean
Domain: NT

For integers x, y >= 2: pi(x+y) <= pi(x) + pi(y), where pi is the prime counting function.

Status: OPEN. Richards (1974) showed this is incompatible with the first HL conjecture. Most experts believe this is FALSE, but no disproof exists. Verified computationally for small ranges.
