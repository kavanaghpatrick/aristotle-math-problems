OPEN GAP: Erdos 251 — Irrationality of sum p_n / 2^n
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/251.lean
Domain: nt

Is the sum ∑ p_n / 2^n irrational, where p_n is the n-th prime?

Prior result (slot699) proved p_n ≤ 2^(n+1) via Bertrand,
p_{n+1} < 2p_n, and conditional summability.

theorem erdos_251 : answer(sorry) ↔
    Irrational (∑' n, (Nat.nth Nat.Prime n : ℝ) / 2^n) := by sorry

Status: OPEN. The sum converges (by prime growth bounds). Its
irrationality is unknown.
