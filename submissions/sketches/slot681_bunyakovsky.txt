OPEN GAP: Bunyakovsky conjecture
Source: formal-conjectures/FormalConjectures/Wikipedia/Bunyakovsky.lean
Domain: NT

If f ∈ ℤ[X] satisfies the Bunyakovsky condition (irreducible, positive leading coeff,
gcd of values = 1), then f(n) is prime for infinitely many natural numbers n.

theorem bunyakovsky_conjecture (f : ℤ[X]) :
    BunyakovskyCondition f ∧ SchinzelCondition {f} →
    Infinite {n : ℕ | (f.eval (n : ℤ)).natAbs.Prime} := by sorry

Status: OPEN since 1857. Implies Dirichlet, twin primes (special cases). No proof known.
