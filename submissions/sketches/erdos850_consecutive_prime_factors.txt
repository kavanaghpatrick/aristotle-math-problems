OPEN GAP: Erdos 850 — Three Consecutive Pairs with Same Prime Factors
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/850.lean
Domain: nt

Do there exist distinct x, y such that x and y share the same prime
factors, x+1 and y+1 share the same prime factors, AND x+2 and y+2
share the same prime factors?

theorem erdos_850 :
    answer(sorry) ↔ ∃ x y : ℕ, x ≠ y ∧ x.primeFactors = y.primeFactors
      ∧ (x + 1).primeFactors = (y + 1).primeFactors
      ∧ (x + 2).primeFactors = (y + 2).primeFactors := by
    sorry

Status: OPEN. The k=2 version (two consecutive pairs) has solutions.
The k=3 version is this open question. No witness or impossibility
proof is known.
