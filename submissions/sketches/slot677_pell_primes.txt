OPEN GAP: Infinitely many prime Pell numbers
Source: formal-conjectures/FormalConjectures/Wikipedia/Pell.lean
Domain: NT

The Pell numbers P_n are defined by P_0=0, P_1=1, P_{n+2}=2P_{n+1}+P_n.
Conjecture: there are infinitely many n such that P_n is prime.
Known primes at indices: 2, 3, 5, 11, 13, 29, 41, 53, 59, 89, 97, ...
