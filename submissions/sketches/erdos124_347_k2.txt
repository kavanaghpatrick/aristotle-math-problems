Let B_d = {∑_{j∈s} d^j : s ⊆ ℕ finite} be the integers with only digits 0,1 in base d.
Consider the pointwise sumset 3^2·B_3 + 4^2·B_4 + 7^2·B_7 = Σ(Pow({3,4,7};2)): the integers
n = a + b + c such that a is a sum of distinct powers 3^j (j ≥ 2), b a sum of distinct powers
4^j (j ≥ 2), c a sum of distinct powers 7^j (j ≥ 2). The claim is that this sumset is
cofinite, with every integer beyond 3,982,888 representable.
