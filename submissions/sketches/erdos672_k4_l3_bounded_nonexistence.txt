OPEN GAP: Erdos 672 — no coprime 4-term AP of 3-full numbers up to 10^11
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/672.lean; Bajpai-Bennett-Chan 2023 (arXiv:2302.03113)
Domain: nt

Erdos asked whether infinitely many coprime 4-term arithmetic progressions of 3-full numbers exist. He conjectured A_infinity(3) = 3 (only finitely many, possibly none). Bajpai-Bennett-Chan (2023) Theorem 1.1 gives under abc the bound gcd(d, N) >> max(d, N)^(3/7 - eps) for any 4-term AP of 3-full numbers. The case (m, k) = (4, 3) is NOT in the exceptional set (1.8) of their paper, so existence/non-existence is open unconditionally. We computed all 18589 3-full numbers up to 10^11 and found 9586 4-term APs among them, NONE with gcd(N, d) = 1 (smallest observed gcd is 216 = 6^3).
