OPEN GAP: Erdős 329 — forward direction (PerfectDifferenceSet embedding ⇒ Sidon density 1)
Source: formal-conjectures/FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/329.lean — theorem erdos_329.of_sub_perfectDifferenceSet
Domain: combinatorics / additive number theory

If every finite Sidon set A ⊆ ℕ embeds in some perfect difference set, then
  sSup { sidonUpperDensity A | IsSidon A } = 1.
This is the forward direction of the Erdős density-1 program. Note: the hypothesis
(every finite Sidon set embeds in a PDS) was disproved in 2025 by Alexeev–Mixon
(arXiv:2510.19804): the Mian–Chowla set {1, 2, 4, 8, 13} does not embed in any
perfect difference set. Hall (1947) gave an earlier counterexample {1, 3, 9, 10, 13}.
So the antecedent is FALSE, and the implication is vacuously true. This is a
direction-closure of one half of the Sidon ↔ PerfectDifferenceSet relationship,
established by 2025 literature; the contrapositive content lies in Erdős 707.
