OPEN GAP: Erdos 258 — Irrationality of Divisor Sum Series
Source: formal-conjectures/FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/258.lean
Domain: nt

Let a_n -> infinity be nonzero naturals. Is sum d(n)/(a_1*...*a_n) irrational?

theorem erdos_258 : answer(sorry) ↔ ∀ (a : ℕ → ℕ), (∀ n, a n ≠ 0) →
    Filter.Tendsto a Filter.atTop Filter.atTop →
    Irrational (∑' (n : ℕ), ((n+1).divisors.card / ∏ i ∈ Finset.Icc 1 n, a i)) := by sorry

Status: OPEN. Monotone case proved by Erdos-Straus. General (non-monotone) case remains open.
