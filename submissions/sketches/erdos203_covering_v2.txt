OPEN GAP: Erdos 203 — Covering system for 2^k * 3^l * m + 1
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/203.lean
Domain: nt

There exists a positive integer m coprime to 6 such that
2^k * 3^l * m + 1 is composite for every k, l >= 0.

Prior result: IF a covering system exists using primes > 3
that covers all residue classes of 2^k * 3^l mod p, THEN such
m exists. The gap is constructing or proving existence of
such a covering system.

theorem erdos_203 :
    ∃ m : ℕ, m > 0 ∧ m.Coprime 6 ∧
    ∀ k l : ℕ, ¬ (2^k * 3^l * m + 1).Prime := by sorry

Status: OPEN. Conditional direction proved. Need covering system.
