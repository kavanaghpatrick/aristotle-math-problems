OPEN GAP: Erdos 677 — LCM of consecutive intervals injective
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/677.lean
Domain: nt

For k > 0 and m >= n+k, is lcm(m+1,...,m+k) ≠ lcm(n+1,...,n+k)?

theorem erdos_677 :
    ∀ (m n k : ℕ), k > 0 → m ≥ n + k →
    Finset.lcmInterval m k ≠ Finset.lcmInterval n k := by sorry

Status: OPEN. Small disjoint equalities for shifted versions include:
lcm(5,6,7)=lcm(14,15), lcm(4,5,6,7)=lcm(20,21), and
lcm(9,10,11,12)=lcm(44,45).
