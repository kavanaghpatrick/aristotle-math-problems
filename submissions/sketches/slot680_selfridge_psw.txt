OPEN GAP: Selfridge/PSW primality conjecture
Source: formal-conjectures/FormalConjectures/Wikipedia/Selfridge.lean
Domain: NT

If p is odd, p ≡ ±2 (mod 5), 2^(p-1) ≡ 1 (mod p), and F_{p+1} ≡ 0 (mod p), then p is prime.
No composite number satisfying all four conditions has ever been found.

theorem selfridge_conjecture (p : ℕ) (hp : IsSelfridge p) : p.Prime := by sorry
