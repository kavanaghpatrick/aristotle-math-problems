OPEN GAP: Erdos 203 — Covering system for 2^k * 3^l * m + 1
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/203.lean
Domain: nt

There exists m > 0 coprime to 6 such that for all k, l >= 0,
the number 2^k * 3^l * m + 1 is never prime.

theorem erdos_203 :
    ∃ m : ℕ, m > 0 ∧ m.Coprime 6 ∧
    ∀ k l : ℕ, ¬ (2^k * 3^l * m + 1).Prime := by sorry

Status: OPEN. Requires constructing a covering system with
primes > 3 covering all residue classes of 2^k * 3^l.
