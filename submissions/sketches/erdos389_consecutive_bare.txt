OPEN GAP: Erdos 389 — Consecutive products divisibility
Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/389.lean
Domain: nt

For every positive integer n, there exists k > 0 such that
the product (n+1)(n+2)...(n+k) divides (n+k+1)...(n+2k).

theorem erdos_389 : ∀ n : ℕ, n ≥ 1 →
    ∃ k : ℕ, k > 0 ∧
    (∏ i in Finset.range k, (n + 1 + i)) ∣
    (∏ i in Finset.range k, (n + k + 1 + i)) := by sorry

Status: OPEN. Known for k=n via central binomial coefficient.
