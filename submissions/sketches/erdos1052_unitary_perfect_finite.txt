Source: FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/1052.lean

Are there only finitely many unitary perfect numbers? A unitary perfect
number n satisfies σ*(n) = 2n where σ* sums divisors d with gcd(d, n/d) = 1.
Only five are known: 6, 60, 90, 87360, 146361946186458562560000.
