OPEN GAP: Erdős 373 — only solution to n! = a!·b! is (10, 7, 6)
Source: formal-conjectures/FormalConjectures/ErdosProblems/373.lean
Domain: nt

It was conjectured (1960s, open since) that the equation n! = a!·b!
with 1 < b ≤ a and a + 1 ≠ n has exactly one solution: (n, a, b) = (10, 7, 6).
Equivalently, the only nontrivial way a factorial equals a product of two
smaller factorials is 10! = 7! · 6! = 3,628,800.
